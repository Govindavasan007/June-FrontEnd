Kanniyakumari Tourist Places

Tiruvalluvar Statuelocation_on

The Thiruvalluvar Statue, or the Valluvar Statue, is a 41-metre-tall(133 ft) stone sculpture of the Tamil poet and philosopher Valluvar, known as Thiruvalluvar, the author of the Thirukkural, an ancient Tamil work on Dharma and morality.

Vivekananda Rock Memoriallocation_on

Vivekananda Rock Memorial is an incredible landmark with a rich history and breathtaking views. The ferry ride to the rock adds to the adventure, offering stunning glimpses of the sea.

Mahatma Gandhi Mandapamlocation_on

The Mahatma Gandhi Memorial, or Gandhi Mandapam, situated on the beach road, adjacent to the sea, stands as a tribute to the father of our nation.
